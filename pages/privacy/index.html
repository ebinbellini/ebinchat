Echeveria Privacy Policy

About this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Echeveria and how we use it. If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Data Echeveria collects

  1. Your account information, such as your password, email, username, profile picture, and your friendlist.
  2. Data that is shared with other users, such as the contents of the messages you send through our service and your friend requests.
  3. Non-user-identifiable information: the number of visitors for different periods and database usage volume.
  4. If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

How we use your information

  1. The A class data, that is account information, is used for logging in, sending password reset emails, displaying your profile picture and username for other users, and so on.
  2. The B class data is not used for anything else than delivering messages and notifications to the users they are sent to. However, the browser usually uses a third party service to deliver the notification.
  3. The C class data is used to monitor server health. We will only use this data to detect if something has gone wrong.
  4. The D class data is hard to say anything about since it could contain anything,
None of the information is shared with third parties, except for the B class data, as explained above.

Cookies

The Echeveria site does not use cookies. The service still works if you disable cookies through a browser setting.

GDPR Data Protection Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:
  1. The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  2. The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete the information you believe is incomplete. This is not applicable as we do not publish information.
  3. The right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  4. The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions. This is not applicable for our service because we do not process your data other than what is necessary in order to provide our service.
  5. The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions. This is not applicable for our service because we do not process your data other than what is necessary in order to provide our service.
  6. The right to data portability – You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

Children's information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. Echeveria does not knowingly collect any personal identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.
